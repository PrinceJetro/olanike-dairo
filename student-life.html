🏆 Champions on & off the Field

Physical fitness and teamwork are core to our development. Our students participate in football, basketball, athletics, and more — building resilience, discipline, and school spirit.

Prefectship & Leadership

We don't just teach — we empower. Our student leadership program places senior students in governance roles, fostering accountability, mentorship, and real-world responsibility. From house captains to press prefects, every leader shapes our community.

  • Leadership workshops & seminars
  • Student parliament sessions
  • Peer mentoring program
