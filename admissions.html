Nurturing the Total Child

Choosing Olanike Dairo Schools means investing in an environment where academic rigor meets emotional intelligence and character development. We welcome students of all backgrounds who are ready to learn, play, and grow into future leaders.

Simple Application Process

1

Inquiry

Visit our campus or call the admissions office to learn about our programs, philosophy, and available spaces.

2

Form Purchase

Obtain the application form online or from the school administrative office (onsite).

3

Assessment

Students take a friendly placement test to ensure proper grade-level alignment.

4

Admission & Enrollment

Successful candidates receive an admission letter and orientation schedule.

Admission Requirements

  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • 2 Recent Passport Photographs
  • Most recent Academic Report from previous school
  • Transfer Certificate (for Secondary students)
  • Immunization / Health Record (for Primary students)
  • Completed application form (online or printed)

Tuition & Fees

Our fee structure is transparent and designed to provide maximum value. We offer:

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement for Primary 1?
Students must be 5 years old by September 1st of the entry year. For other levels, placement is based on academic assessment.
Do you offer school bus services?
Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation within Obalende, Lagos Island, and select neighboring areas. Routes are planned for convenience and safety.
Is there an entrance examination?
Yes, we conduct a friendly placement assessment (literacy, numeracy, and general aptitude) to ensure your child is placed in the grade that best suits their academic development.
Are scholarships available?
We offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional students and need-based support. Contact admissions for more details on the scholarship application process.
What is the admission timeline?
Admissions run year-round, but the main intake begins in September. Early application is advised as spaces are limited per class.
