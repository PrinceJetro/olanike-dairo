The Calling

Foundations of Purpose

Born and raised in Lagos, Olanike Dairo witnessed firsthand the gaps in traditional education — where rote learning overshadowed creativity. Her early years as a volunteer teacher ignited a deep passion: to create a space where every child's innate curiosity is celebrated. She pursued advanced studies in Educational Psychology, determined to reshape how young minds engage with knowledge.

"Education isn't about filling a bucket; it's about lighting a fire that burns for a lifetime."
The Vision

Seeing a Future of Possibility

After years of research and observing both local and international best practices, Olanike synthesized a unique pedagogical model: blending disciplined academics with unstructured, curiosity-driven play. She envisioned a school where students would not only excel in exams but also grow emotionally, socially, and ethically — a true "total child" approach.

The Birth

A School is Born

In 2008, with just a handful of students and an unwavering belief, Olanike Dairo Children Centre opened its doors in Obalende. The early days were demanding, but the radical results — joyful, confident learners — spoke for themselves. Soon, the school expanded from a nursery to a full-fledged primary and secondary institution, becoming a beacon of holistic education in Lagos Island.

The Legacy

Growth & Lasting Impact

Today, Olanike Dairo Schools stands as a testament to one woman's vision. With over 1,200 alumni excelling in universities and careers worldwide, the school's philosophy of "Play & Hard Work" has influenced a generation. Beyond academics, the institution remains deeply rooted in community upliftment, offering scholarships and mentorship programs across Obalende and beyond.
